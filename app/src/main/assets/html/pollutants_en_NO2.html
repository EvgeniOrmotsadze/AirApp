NO2
Nitrogen Dioxide
Description
reddish-brown, highly reactive gas
Sources
Motor vehicles, electric facilitates and other industrial, commercial and residential facilities combusting liquid fuels
Health Effects
Susceptibility to respiratory infections, lung irritation and respiratory symptoms (for example cough, chest pain, difficulties breathing)
